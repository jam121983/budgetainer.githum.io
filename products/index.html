Africa Tanks Product Range

We offer a wide range of sizes to fit your needs

Africa Tanks are proud to offer several colours and sizes of tanks to meet our client’s every need. We offer regular water tanks with volumes of 5000, 4000, 2500 and 2000 litres.

For customers that order quantities of more than 800 tanks, we can personalize their tanks with their name embossed in the moulding of the tank.

We also manufacture a 1600 litre under-eave tank, for backup water and rain water harvesting that is of ergonomic design and has a pleasing appearance. These tanks will be manufactured in two shades of grey and a sand colour to fit into most estate’s house rules. It is designed to fit next to a house and under the eve of the house`s roof.

We will also manufacture a brand new tank with a capacity of 2500 litre that will be split near the top of the tank. This will be for the economical market as it will be cheaper to transport and will be a great value offer for the rural and export market.

Tanks available in the following colours

Any colour can be manufactured on a client’s request – with a min order requirement of 150 tanks.

3 Layered tank construction

Our tanks make use of a three layer profile that makes for a supremely strong tank.

 

Outer Layer

The outer layer is UV stabilised – the HDPE for the outer layer has been modified to ensure a high resistance to UV degradation.

Middle Layer

The middle is black, and blocks out sunlight or UV radiation and that ensures that there is no algae and bacteria growth inside the tank

Inner Layer

The inner layer is white-opaque. This makes inspection of the inside of the tank both easy and less of a scary affair. This layer is also made from a food grade HDPE material ensuring safe water for consumption.

How our products are made

Africa Tanks use a worldwide proven manufacturing process called Extrusion Blow moulding. The material used in this manufacturing process is HDPE (High density poly-ethylene) and is a very strong and durable material. 

Industry standard outlets & inlets

All of our tanks come with industry standard outlets and inlets. They also have an added safety feature in that their lids are screw on, thus preventing anything accidentally falling into the tank.
